Services

The Islamic Foundation of Toronto strives to play a vital role in building a strong and vibrant Muslim community. Through our services and programs, we hope to foster a sense of unity and support among our members. Our commitment to service reinforces the masjid as a hub for social justice, spiritual development, and collective action.

Funerals

Losing a loved one is truly heartbreaking. We are here to help you with everything from bathing to burial services.

Rentals

Whether it’s a wedding, Aqiqa or any other private event, the gymnasium and cafeteria are available to book.

Marriages

We provide imams who are licensed marriage officers to perform your Nikah and take care of all the paperwork on your special day.

Working for the
generations to come

The Islamic Foundation’s religious schools provide a welcoming environment for students to gain an excellent Islamic education while deepening their connection with the Quran and Sunnah.
